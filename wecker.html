Wecker app

Sept 2017

Tags: interface design

toolkit: Processing Axure RP PS Sketch

The Sound Wecker APP, which i designed, is not like the normal wecker APP. When the alarm ring, a Soundtrack will show. And you have to delete the Soundtrack, then you can close the alarm. If you choose to 'sleep 15min or 20min more', a Soundtrack will be added every internal. In this case, every Soundtrack will play together. The longer you choose to sleep, the more Soundtrack will be added, and the noisier it will be.

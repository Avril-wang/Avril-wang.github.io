Just In Cart

Juli 2016

Tags: Interface design

toolkit: Rhino PS Sketching

'Just in Cart' is a shopping system, which aims to make the shopping process more convienient. It includes two main parts. The first part is the weighing system. The electronic scale is at the same time a basket for fruit and vegetables . After taking the amount of vegetables you want, the price will show directly on the electronic scale. The second part is the shopping app. The user can scan the barcode, and then pay for the goods. During the system, the shopping time will be saved.

Dear Data

May 2018

Tags: Information design

toolkit: sketching PS

'Dear Data'is a year-long, analog data drawing project by information designer Giorgia Lupi and Stefaine Posave. Followed them, by collecting and drawing my personal data, i want to reconsider my life from a totally different perspectiv. Here are the six weeks life-tracking.Themes are: yawning, chatting, e-dictionary, my desk, Route in Greece, take a photo.
